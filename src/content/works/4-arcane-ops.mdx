## Overview

<div class="hr-div"></div>

Arcane Ops was a limited-run paranormal podcast that ran for seven episodes at the end of 2020. We designed the website layout in Adobe XD and built it using WordPress, WooCommerce, and Bootstrap. We designed the entire brand identity, including logos, social media images, video animations, and video thumbnails in Adobe Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop, AfterEffects, and Premiere.
