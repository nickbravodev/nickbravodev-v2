## Overview

<div class="hr-div"></div>

Fortify 24x7 asked us to design a series of half-page magazine ads to be included in several issues of Unredacted Magazine. After receiving the parameters and copy, we designed the ads in Adobe Illustrator. Since the magazine is an Interactive PDF, the final designs were embedded directly into the InDesign document with multiple targeted hyperlinks. These ads can be seen in various issues of Unredacted Magazine.
